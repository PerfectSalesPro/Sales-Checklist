Immediate Actions (Post-Signature)

0%
  • Verify the shipping address and the install address, confirming if they are different.
  • Ask your champion:
    "Will you be the main point of contact for scheduling the installation, or is there an office manager or someone else I should coordinate with?"
    • If there is another POC, be sure to get their full name, email, direct phone number, and their role/title.
  • Add any new contact to the account immediately.
  • For all contacts associated with the opportunity, assign their relevant persona (e.g., Business Owner, Fleet Manager, Office Manager/Dispatch).
  • Navigate to the opportunity record.
  • Hit Capture Customer Details.
  • Pick install/shipping address.
  • Input all Vehicle Identification Numbers (VINs), click "Validate VINs," and ensure successful validation.
  • Hit the Finalize button.
  • Open the relevant call recording in Gong.
  • Thoroughly fill out the Pain, Vision, Power, Value, and Consensus (PVPVC) framework.
  • Note: This is required for all sales, including Sit Sat Solds (SSS).

Post-Sale Confirmed (Within 24 Hours)

0%
  • On the opportunity record, confirm the M2 indicator is green.
  • If it's red, investigate and resolve the underlying issue.
  • Update FLW3 1 Stop Shop.
  • Critical: First, verify the customer is enrolled in the correct cadence.
    • Check the account in Salesloft to ensure the "2025_WOS_COJ_Welcome_VCMI" cadence is active.
    • If it's missing, highlight the entire account line in red on the "FLW3 1 Stop Shop" sheet to flag it for a manager.
  • In Salesloft, locate the task labeled "Welcome SMS #1".
  • Copy the text from the task description below:

    Welcome to the Verizon Connect team! To kick things off, here's a quick welcome from our GM on how we'll get you to results, fast. https://verizonconnect.com/video/welcome

  • Click "Log & Complete" in Salesloft to send the text and ensure the cadence proceeds automatically.
  • This must be done within 24 hours.

Follow-Up Actions (Within 24-48 Hours Post-Sale)

After the COJ text message is sent and the VOSS team has sent the fleet confirmation email (which you are CC'd on), follow up with the customer.

Instruct them to find and respond to the email with the subject line:

"Quick Action Needed: Confirm Fleet & Shipping Details"

Ask them to review the details in the email and reply with "Looks good" or similar to confirm everything is correct.
